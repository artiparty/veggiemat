Like A Coloring Book of Deliciousness

Crazy colors, odd textures, and funny flavors: it’s understandable that vegetables seem so scary. But you can help your children discover a veggie wonderland, learning healthy eating can also be fun.

How many colors
will they eat?!

  1. Prepare Your Veggies

    Show your kids where veggies come from, how to wash them, and fun shapes to cut them into.

  2. Fill Out the VeggieMat™

    Match the veggie pieces to the shapes, discuss the colors, and let your kids discover at their own pace.

  3. Build a Healthy Habit

    Help your kids love veggies earlier to increase the likelihood of a healthier lifestyle when they grow up.

There’s Science on Every Mat

VeggieMat™ is more than just mealtime fun: We’ve used four scientific studies in designing our mats to ensure every detail helps your kids eat more vegetables.

  • Involvement Encourages Eating

    Read the research

    Children who help prepare their meals, eat up to 76% more vegetables than those who don’t.

  • Colors Stimulate Interest

    Read the research

    Kids love variety, wanting as many as six different colors on their plate (in contrast to us boring adults who prefer just three!)

  • Playing Leads to Trying

    Read the research

    Playing with food actually helps pickier eaters try new things (but you might want to keep this a secret…).

  • Visual Appeal Is Important

    Read the research

    Kids eat more tasty-looking fruit than regular-looking fruit, and we’re sure it’s the same for veggies.

Hassle-free & Safety-assured

  • Great Table Grip

    Concerned the food might end up on the floor? VeggieMat™ is harder to move than regular plates. Sprinkle some drops of water under it to make it stick to the table even better.

  • Dishwasher Safe

    When there are kids involved, you need all the help you can get. We’ve made VeggieMat™ dishwasher-safe, so there’s one less thing for you to worry about.

  • Approved Materials

    We use non-toxic, food-safe FDA and LFGB-approved materials in our mats — nothing but the best for your kids.
