Hassle-free

Easy To Clean

When there are kids involved, you need all the help you can get! Simply wash VeggieMat™ with warm soapy water or clean it in the dishwasher.

Great Table Grip

Concerned the food might end up on the floor? VeggieMat™ is harder to move than regular plates. Sprinkle some drops of water under it to make it stick to the table even more.

Easy to Store and Carry

Being elastic and foldable, VeggieMat™ is super-flexible for storing and carrying. It can be stored rolled up or folded, occupying minimal space in a drawer or cupboard.

Eco-Friendly Money Saver

VeggieMat™ is more than just mealtime fun – Made of the same materials as reusable baking mats, it is also an eco-friendly reusable alternative to disposable parchment paper for rolling dough while baking cakes, puddings, bread, pizzas, etc. for a more sustainable kitchen, to save money and to keep the environment clean.
